Sakamoto Days

The story follows Taro Sakamoto, a former hitman who was once considered the best in the world. After falling in love and meeting a woman, Sakamoto retires from crime and works at a convenience store with his wife and child. However, his enemies and former partners show up to take him out. Sakamoto must find creative ways to subdue his enemies without killing them.
