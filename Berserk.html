Berserk

Berserk is a story that follows the story of Guts; a swordsman whose very existence has been Hell since he was born. Guts ends up suffering through a lot of traumatic events but ends up finding two opportunities to rise above his trauma. The first time is when he joins up with a mercenary company named The Band of the Hawk, led by a man named Griffith. Griffith is a man many would willingly follow into Hell but who no one understands. Through his interactions with Griffith and other members of the Band of the Hawk like Casca (his future love interest) and Judea, Guts decides that he wants to find his own reason for living after he helps see Griffith's dream come close to fruition. A year after leaving Guts finds out that Griffith had done something that got him imprisoned and the Band of the Hawk branded as criminals. He reunites with his fellow mercenaries, confides in Casca at a moment where they are most vulnerable, and helps rescue Griffith from prison. Guts is maimed and left with the need to gain revenge on Griffith and those who he works with from the world beyond Earth.

Characters
Manga Highlights

Guts

Guts, renowned as the "Black Swordsman", is a former mercenary and branded wanderer who travels the world in a constant internal struggle between pursuing his own ends and upholding his attachments to those dear to him.

Casca

Casca is a former unit commander of the Band of the Falcon and an estranged companion of Guts.

Griffith

Griffith, renowned as the "White Falcon", is the leader of the reborn Band of the Falcon and Supreme Commander of the Midland Regular Army. Famed for his leadership in numerous battles, he is celebrated as a savior across the continent and revered as the "Falcon of Light".
