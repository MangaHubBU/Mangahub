Bleach

Ichigo Kurosaki is a high school student who can see ghosts and is attacked by a Hollow, a hungry and tormented spirit. Rukia Kuchiki, a Shinigami (Death God), saves Ichigo and his sisters, but is injured in the fight. Rukia transfers her powers to Ichigo, who must then train to become a Soul Reaper and maintain balance between the living and the dead.
